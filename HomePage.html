Welcome to Simply Taken Photography!

Welcome to Simply Taken Photography! My name is Charley Varnum, and my photography journey began in 2012 when I first picked up a camera. Many memories and countless snapshots later, I'm here to share my passion with you. By day, I'm the Office Manager at Global Graphics, a local business specializing in screen printing and signage. It's a role that keeps me busy, but my heart has never drifted far from photography. Now, I'm thrilled to be rekindling my passion and diving back into what truly lights me up. Since starting Simply Taken Photography in 2020, I've had the honor of capturing the genuine emotions and timeless moments of countless individuals and families in our community. Each click of the shutter has allowed me to freeze fleeting moments in time, creating memories that will last forever. If you're interested in booking a session, I'm just a message, call, or email away. I'm genuinely excited about the prospect of working with you in the near future, capturing moments that you'll treasure for a lifetime.
